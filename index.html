About Saitama

Saitama is the most powerful chara in the series! Become a hero is his dream. His assigned name is Caped Baldy of hero association. And for some reason, he is still ranked 7 in B-class hero.

Saitama's Characteristics

  1. He's an ordinary looking bald man.
  2. He is humble and hero like. Fubuki said 'he attracts the strong'.
  3. He can kill any enemy with one punch.
  4. He desires for an exciting and challenging fight.
